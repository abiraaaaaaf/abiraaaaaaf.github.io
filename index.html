About Me

Hi! My name is Fariba Lotfi. I am really interested in Artificial intelligence and its posibilities!
I'm a Ph.D Candidate at the department of Computer Engineering, Sharif University of Technology. My research interest is Machine Learning, especially Deep Learning in Image Processing tasks. I'm working as a research assistant under the supervision of Prof. Mansour Jamzad and Prof. Hamid Beigy. My Ph.D. dissertation is about automatic image annotation using deep learning. Currently, I'm working on image annotation using graph neural networks. My Master dissertation was about community question answering using deep learning, specifically duplicate question detection. Also, My bachelor dissertation was about an environment with multi-agents with the duty of pushing a box using Q-Learning.
